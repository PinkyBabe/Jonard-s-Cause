Jonard's Cause
Joy
Jonard feels immense joy when he is with his friends. Their shared moments and laughter uplift him and fill his heart with happiness. The connection with his friends brings out the best in him.
Excitement
The thrill of new adventures with his friends excites Jonard. Whether it's the rush of excitement on trips or the joy of planning together, these moments fill him with energy and joy.
Sadness
But when Jonard is alone, a deep sadness sets in. His insecurities weigh him down, making him question his worth. It's a battle he faces within, where his own mind becomes a prison.
Anger
Jonard’s anger often comes from within. His frustration with himself, his struggles with self-image, and the pressure to meet expectations all fuel his anger. It’s an inner conflict he tries to overcome.
